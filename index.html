Privacy Policy for Focus Nest

Last Updated: 23.03.2026

1. Introduction

Focus Nest ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we treat information when you use our mobile application. Focus Nest is designed as a "Privacy First" app, meaning we prioritize keeping your data on your device.

2. Data Collection & Usage

Personal Data: Focus Nest does not collect, store, or transmit any personal identification information (such as your name, email address, or phone number) to our servers. We do not require account registration.

Local Data: All your focus sessions, statistics, and settings are stored locally on your device using standard platform storage. We do not have access to this data, and it is not backed up to our servers.

Location Data: Focus Nest does not collect, use, or share your precise or approximate geographical location.

Device Information: We may access basic, non-identifiable device information (such as model and OS version) through Capacitor plugins to ensure technical compatibility and performance.

3. Payments & Subscriptions

Transactions are processed securely via Google Play Billing. We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. While RevenueCat processes transaction data, we do not have access to your financial details. RevenueCat provides us with anonymous lifecycle events to enable "Elite" features.

4. Third-Party Advertising

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements in the Free version. AdMob may collect and use service-related information:

  • Google Advertising ID (AAID) for ad personalization and frequency capping.
  • Basic device telemetry for ad delivery and fraud prevention.

Note: You can manage your ad preferences in your Android device settings (Settings > Google > Ads).

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Because all progress data is stored locally on your device, we retain it only as long as the app is installed. You can permanently delete all local data at any time by using the "Reset Progress" feature in the app settings or by uninstalling the application.

6. Data Sharing & Security

We do not sell or trade your data. We implement industry-standard security measures provided by the Android operating system (including Sandboxing and Local Storage encryption) to protect your information.

7. Children's Privacy

Focus Nest does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. Our application is a productivity tool intended for a general audience.
