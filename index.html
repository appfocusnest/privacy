Privacy Policy Focus Nest

Last Updated: April 01, 2026

1. Data Controller

The data controller is Joanna Maleszka, an independent developer based in Poland, operating the Focus Nest application (“Controller”).

Contact: app.focusnest@gmail.com

2. Overview and "No-Account" Policy

Focus Nest is a “Privacy First” application. Focus Nest does not require user accounts. No personal profiles are created or stored on our servers.

3. Data Collection and App Permissions

3.1 Personal Data

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or other directly identifying personal information.

3.2 Local Data Storage

All focus sessions, settings, and analytics (such as session duration and integrity scores) are stored locally on your device using Android system storage (e.g., SharedPreferences, WebView Local Storage). This data is not transmitted to our servers and remains under your control.

3.2.1 System Backups

If Android Backup is enabled, app data may be encrypted and stored within your personal Google Drive backup. The Controller does not access, control, or process these backups.

3.3 Android Permissions

  • Foreground Service: To keep the timer active during background sessions.
  • Exact Alarms: To trigger session completion at the precise scheduled time.
  • Wake Lock & Full Screen Intent: To display completion screens on locked devices.
  • Disable Keyguard: To allow stopping alarms without fully unlocking the device.
  • Notifications: To deliver alerts and session completion reminders.
  • Vibration: To provide haptic feedback for timer events.
  • Network & Internet: Required for purchase validation and advertising services.

3.4 Technical Data & Identifiers

Certain technical information may be automatically processed by third-party SDKs to provide core services. This includes device model, OS version, Advertising ID (AAID), IP address, and purchase tokens. Usage statistics used for in-app analytics are processed locally on your device.

4. Third-Party Services & International Transfers

The App uses the following third-party providers:

  • Google (Google Play Services, AdMob) – Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat (Purchase validation and licensing) – Privacy Policy

Data may be transferred to the United States. Such transfers rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and/or the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework to ensure a high level of data protection.

5. Advertising Disclosure (Google AdMob)

We display personalized ads through Google AdMob. This involves processing identifiers like the AAID and IP address for ad delivery and fraud prevention.

For users in the European Union, consent is requested via Google UMP (IAB TCF 2.2). We honor Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals. You can opt out at any time via your device settings or Ad Preferences.

6. AI & Automated Decisions

Focus Nest does not use your data for AI model training or automated decision-making with legal effects.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 (16 in EU). No personal data is knowingly collected. If identified, such data will be deleted immediately.

8. Legal Basis (GDPR)

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) – for personalized advertising.
  • Contractual Necessity (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) – to provide and validate Elite premium features.
  • Legitimate Interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) – for app functionality, fraud prevention, and security.

9. User Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, or erase your data. Since most data is local, you can exercise these rights by clearing app storage or uninstalling the App.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

We do not sell personal data. Certain advertising activities may be considered “sharing”. California residents may request access, deletion, or opt-out by contacting us at app.focusnest@gmail.com.

10. Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until deletion. Third-party providers retain advertising and purchase data only as long as necessary under their respective policies.

11. RevenueCat Disclosure

RevenueCat processes purchase receipts solely for license validation. No credit card information is collected by the Controller. RevenueCat acts as a data processor under GDPR.

12. Data Security

We use standard Android security mechanisms (sandboxing) and HTTPS/TLS encryption for all communications with third-party providers.
