Privacy Policy Focus Nest

Last Updated: April 19, 2026

1. Data Controller

The data controller is Joanna Maleszka, an independent developer based in Poland, operating the Focus Nest application (“Controller”).

📧 Contact: app.focusnest@gmail.com

2. No-Account Policy

Focus Nest does not require user accounts. No registration is required and no user profiles are created. No server-side storage is used by the developer. Third-party services may process data on their own infrastructure. All core functionality operates without identity tracking.

3. Data Processing Overview Data Safety

This section explicitly defines data categories for Google Play Data Safety compliance.

3.1 Data NOT Collected by the App (App-controlled data)

The App does NOT collect or transmit: Name, Email address, Phone number, Contacts, Precise location, Photos or files. No such data is processed by the Controller.

3.2 Data Stored Locally on Device (NOT collected by the Controller)

The following data is stored only on the user’s device: Focus session timers, App settings, Session statistics (e.g. duration, session completion status). This data is NOT transmitted to servers, NOT accessible by the developer, and NOT shared externally by the app code itself.

3.3 Data Collected by Third Parties (Independent Controllers)

Some data is processed by third-party SDKs acting as independent controllers:

  • Google AdMob: May process Advertising ID (AAID), IP address, Device identifiers, and Ad interaction data for ad delivery, fraud prevention, and analytics.
  • RevenueCat: May process purchase receipts, transaction identifiers, and subscription status for validation and premium feature activation.

4. Data Sharing

The App does not transmit data to developer-controlled servers. However, third-party SDKs (AdMob, RevenueCat) may collect and share data independently as described in their own policies.

5. Android Permissions

The App requests only necessary permissions for functionality:

  • Foreground Service – Timer operation in background.
  • Exact Alarms – Precise session timing.
  • Notifications – Session alerts.
  • Wake Lock & Full Screen Intent – Completion display on locked devices.
  • Vibration – Haptic feedback.
  • Network & Internet – Ads and purchase validation.

6. Advertising (Google AdMob Compliance)

The App uses Google AdMob for ad personalization (only with consent), non-personalized ads, and fraud prevention.

EU Consent: For users in the EEA, consent is collected via Google UMP (IAB TCF 2.2 framework). Users can accept or reject personalized ads. If consent is refused, only non-personalized ads are shown and the App remains fully usable.

7. Legal Basis (GDPR)

Processing is based on: Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)), Contract necessity (Art. 6(1)(b)), and Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)).

8. Data Retention

Local data remains on device until deleted or app uninstall. No server-side storage is used by the developer. Third-party retention is governed by their privacy policies.

9. Data Deletion

Users may delete all local data by uninstalling the App or clearing app storage. No external deletion request is required because no server-side accounts exist.

10. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or 16 in EU). No intentional data collection from minors occurs.

11. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob – Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat – Privacy Policy

12. International Data Transfers

Data processed by third-party providers may be transferred outside the EEA (including the United States) under Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

13. Security Measures

We apply Android application sandboxing, HTTPS/TLS encrypted communication with third parties, and minimal data collection principle.

14. AI / Automated Decision-Making

The App does NOT use AI to process personal data, perform automated decisions with legal effects, or use user data for machine learning training.

15. User Rights (GDPR + CCPA)

Users have the right to access personal data (if any exists in third-party systems), request deletion, restrict processing, or withdraw consent. Since the App does not use accounts, these rights are exercised via device controls or third-party provider settings.

16. Data Safety Alignment Statement

This policy is aligned with Google Play Data Safety disclosure requirements regarding collected, shared, and processed data.
