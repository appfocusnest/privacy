Privacy Policy for Focus Nest

Last Updated: March 23, 2026

1. Data Controller

The data controller is the creator of the Focus Nest application (“Controller”).

Contact: app.focusnest@gmail.com

2. Overview

Focus Nest is designed as a “Privacy First” application. We do not require account registration, do not store user data on our servers, and keep data locally on your device whenever possible.

3. Data Collection and Use

3.1 Personal Data

We do not collect, store, or process personal data such as name, email, or phone number.

3.2 Local Data

All focus sessions, statistics, and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data.

3.3 Technical Data

We may process limited non-identifiable technical data such as device model and operating system version solely to ensure proper functionality.

4. Third-Party Services (Processors)

We use the following third-party services: Google (Google Play, AdMob) for payments, advertising, and fraud prevention; and RevenueCat for purchase management and premium feature validation. These providers may process data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Advertising

We use Google AdMob. AdMob may process your advertising ID (AAID), device data, and ad interaction data. You can manage ad preferences in your Android settings.

6. Payments

Payments are processed by Google Play. We do not have access to your payment details. RevenueCat may process anonymous transaction data to validate Elite features.

7. Legal Basis (GDPR)

Where applicable, data processing is based on legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) for ensuring functionality, security, and improvement of the App.

8. International Data Transfers

Some data may be processed outside the European Economic Area. Appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC) are used by our service providers.

9. Data Retention

Local data is stored until deleted by the user. Third-party data is retained according to the providers’ respective policies.

10. User Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access your data, request correction or deletion, restrict processing, object to processing, and lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

11. Data Security

We rely on Android security mechanisms, including sandboxing and local data encryption, to protect your information.

12. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

13. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes become effective upon publication.
