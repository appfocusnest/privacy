Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 08.03.2026

1. Introduction

Focus Nest ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we treat information when you use our mobile application.

2. Data Collection & Usage

Personal Data: Focus Nest does not collect, store, or transmit any personal identification information (such as your name, email address, or phone number) to our servers.

Payment Data: All financial transactions are processed securely by Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store your credit card or bank details.

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements to users in the Free version. AdMob may collect and use service-related information:

  • Google's advertising ID from your device.
  • Cookies and usage data to personalize ads.

4. Analytics

We may use basic, anonymous analytics to understand how many users use the app. This data is fully anonymized and does not identify you.

5. User Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the EU, you have the right to access the data stored on your device or delete your data via the app settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Focus Nest does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.
